Why Use Structured Headings?

Improved Readability

Using headings appropriately ensures that your content is easy to read and navigate. Readers can quickly find the sections they're interested in without scanning through unnecessary text.

Enhanced Accessibility

Proper heading structure also benefits screen readers and assistive technologies, ensuring that your content is accessible to a wider audience.
